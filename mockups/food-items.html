Food Item Styles — 16-Bit

Two art styles for the same food items. Style A uses bold dark outlines (classic SNES). Style B uses color shading with no hard outlines (modern pixel). A sample from each of the four levels is shown.

Style A — Bold Outlined
Thick #222 outlines on every item. Flat interior fills with minimal shading. Strong silhouette — items are instantly readable even at small sizes. Classic SNES / GBA RPG icon feel.

No hard outlines. Items use gradient fills, subtle highlights, and drop shadows for depth. Softer, warmer feel. Modern pixel art like Stardew Valley or Celeste pickup items. Feels more organic and less "icon-like."
